Season 1 In the first episode they introduce Teddy as he is riding the train into Sweetwater. He spies Delores with a far-seeing vision and meets up with her and she says, “You came back”. They want to present the pair as host and visitor. Bernard asks “what do you think about the guests” and they cut to Teddy, riding in on the train, sitting near guests discussing their previous visits.
