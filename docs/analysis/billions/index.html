Versus

Axelrod
Bobby Axelrod represents the struggle within the voluntary system of capitalist production.
Rhoades
Chuck Rhoades represents social control by political pull. In this system all wealth is taken as a given and the question is merely how to equitably distribute it.

The series explores one of the most controversial issues of our time: “inequality”, aka: wealth.

Billions Season One (2016)

Season One sets up the two opposing forces: The motive of self-improvement through prosperity vs. the motive of societal control towards some kind of "greater good".

The ideals are starkly laid out. Two entirely opposed ideas of the good, and the good life, and good actions, in the most general sense of the word “good”.
